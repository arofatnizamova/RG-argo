Founded in 1981, Rahim Gardizi Group of Companies has emerged as a leading force in Afghanistan’s metals and fertilizer industries. With operations extending across Afghanistan, the Middle East, and Central Asia, RAHIM GARDIZI GROUP employs approximately 500 skilled professionals and specializes in the steel, mineral, and organic fertilizer sectors, as well as hydrocarbon products, supported by its subsidiary operations in Uzbekistan.

Our Reputation

RAHIM GARDIZI GROUP is widely recognized for its integrity, reliability, and innovative business models, supported by a robust financial foundation. We prioritize long-term partnerships with international organizations and NGOs, focusing on sustainable growth and delivering enduring value across our operational footprint. Our dedication to sustainability is evident in our corporate processes and initiatives that contribute positively to society.   

Our Achievements 

  • Market Leadership in Steel: RAHIM GARDIZI GROUP holds the largest share of Afghanistan’s steel market, securing exclusive contracts with leading manufacturers in the region. We proudly supply over 70% of the country’s steel demand. 
  • Fertilizer Industry Leadership: As one of Afghanistan’s largest suppliers of mineral fertilizers, RAHIM GARDIZI GROUP maintains strong relationships with global manufacturers such as Phosagro. In 2023, we successfully delivered 50,000 metric tons of Diammonium Phosphate (DAP) and Urea fertilizer to the United Nations FAO, continuing as their largest supplier for the second consecutive year. 

Commercial Excellence 

Rahim Gardizi Group dominance extends across multiple essential sectors, including steel and fertilizers. As the largest supplier of steel in Afghanistan, we meet over 70% of the country’s demand by leveraging exclusive contracts and strategic partnerships with top regional manufacturers. This achievement underscores our pivotal role in supporting Afghanistan’s infrastructure and industrial growth with a consistent and reliable supply of high-quality metal products.
